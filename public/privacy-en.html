Privacy Policy

Effective: January 1, 2026

DreamBible ("the Service") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • When you sign in with Google: your name, email address, and profile photo
  • Dream entries: text you enter and AI-generated reflections (stored on your device only, not sent to our servers)
  • Payment information: processed by Stripe — we never store your card details
  • Usage data: monthly interpretation count (stored on your device)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and manage your account
  • To provide AI-powered dream reflections (via Gemini API)
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions

3. Third-Party Services

We share only the minimum necessary information with:

  • Google (OAuth sign-in)
  • Supabase (authentication and data storage)
  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Google Gemini (AI reflection generation — your dream text is sent)

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Dream entries are stored on your device. Deleting the app removes them. Account data is deleted immediately upon request.
